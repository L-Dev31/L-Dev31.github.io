I am an independent designer and developer sitting at the intersection of logic and aesthetics. I help clients build digital identities that are not just seen, they are experienced.

My foundation is not digital. It started in Provence with clay and crayons. Creation was never a hobby for me, it was my first language for understanding the world.

From self-teaching GameMaker and producing videos to modding and web development, I learned to build both the visual layer and the system behind it.

I advocate for a T-shaped perspective: design at the core, with curiosity across code, politics, history, and culture.
