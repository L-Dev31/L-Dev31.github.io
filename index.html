My Story

Early Life & Origins

Born in Grasse to Albanian and Dutch parents. Inspired by my grandmother, I taught myself art and coding from age 10.

Caribbean Entrepreneurship

At age 14, I moved to Guadeloupe. By 17, I founded "Rayon," a community marketplace for buying and selling products and real estate across the French Caribbean islands, applying my self-taught skills to real-world business.

Professional Development

In 2025, I decided to return to the French Riviera to formalize my expertise. I am currently pursuing my studies in Digital Project Management and Graphic Design at Sophia Ynov Campus.
