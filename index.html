My Story

Early Life

Born in Grasse to Albanian and Dutch parents. Inspired by my grandmother, I taught myself art and coding from age 10.

Caribbean Entrepreneurship

At 14, I moved to Guadeloupe. At 17, I founded Rayon, the first community marketplace for products and real estate in the French West Indies. As the region's youngest tech entrepreneur, I’ve scaled a platform that connects thousands of users across 4 islands, bridging the gap in a $15B+ regional market.

Professional Development

In 2025, I decided to return to the French Riviera to formalize my expertise. I am currently pursuing my studies in Digital Project Management and Graphic Design at Sophia Ynov Campus.
