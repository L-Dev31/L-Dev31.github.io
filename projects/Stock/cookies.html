Cookie Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Nemeris is designed to work without tracking you.

Cookies

Nemeris does not set advertising or analytics cookies, and does not use cookies to track you across sites.

Local storage (functional)

Instead of cookies, Nemeris uses your browser's localStorage to remember information that is strictly necessary for the app to function, your portfolio, transactions, and settings (such as your profile name and your data-proxy URL). This information stays on your device, is not sent to us, and is not used for tracking. You can remove it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-party requests

When the app loads, your browser fetches fonts and code libraries from third-party content delivery networks (Google Fonts, Font Awesome, jsDelivr) and market data from a data proxy / Yahoo Finance. These providers may log technical request data such as your IP address as part of delivering their service. They are not used by Nemeris to place tracking cookies.
