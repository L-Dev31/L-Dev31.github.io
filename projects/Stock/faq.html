Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 12 June 2026

What is Nemeris?

Nemeris is a free web app for tracking your portfolio, reading clear stock analysis and quantitative metrics, and exploring markets with real-time charts. It's built to make the market easier to understand for everyday investors, clarity first, not an overwhelming wall of data.

Is Nemeris financial advice?

No. Nemeris gives you information and tools, not advice or personal recommendations. The automated "signals" are technical calculations on historical data, not predictions. Please read the disclaimer and always do your own research.

Where does the market data come from?

From Yahoo Finance. Data can be delayed or occasionally unavailable, and is not guaranteed to be accurate or complete.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up. You can start using Nemeris immediately.

Where is my portfolio stored? Is it private?

Your portfolio and settings are stored locally in your own browser, they are not uploaded to us. Because of this, your data lives on the device you use. Clearing your browser's site data will remove it. See the privacy policy for details.

Does Nemeris cost anything?

Nemeris is currently free to use.

Why isn't any data showing?

Nemeris fetches market data through a data proxy. If charts are empty, check the data-proxy ("Worker") URL in Settings, or try again later, the data source may be temporarily rate-limited.

How do I get in touch?

For questions or feedback, contact contact@nemeris.app.
