Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This policy explains how Nemeris ("we", "the Service") handles information when you use the app. We have designed Nemeris to be privacy-friendly by default: there are no user accounts, and your data stays on your own device.

Who is responsible

The data controller for the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is the operator of Nemeris. For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact: contact@nemeris.app.

What data we process

Nemeris does not require you to register and does not collect a name, email, or payment information on our servers. The following data is created and stored locally in your browser (using localStorage) and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your portfolio and transactions (tickers, quantities, prices, dates you enter).
  • App settings, including an optional profile name, an optional profile picture, and an optional custom data-proxy ("Worker") URL.

This information remains on your device until you clear it (for example by clearing your browser storage).

Third parties that receive data when you use the app

  • Market-data provider (Yahoo Finance), via a proxy. To display quotes and charts, the app requests market data. These requests (e.g. the tickers you view) are routed through a data proxy you configure, and reach Yahoo Finance's servers, which are located outside the EU (United States). Such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards under the GDPR.
  • Content delivery networks. The app loads fonts and libraries from third-party CDNs (Google Fonts, Font Awesome, jsDelivr). These providers may process technical data such as your IP address and browser type in order to deliver those files.

Analytics & tracking

Nemeris does not currently use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking cookies. If this changes, this policy and a consent mechanism will be added before any such tracking is enabled.

Legal basis

Where the GDPR applies, we process the limited technical data necessary to deliver the Service on the basis of our legitimate interest in providing a functioning application, and on the basis of your consent where required (for example, any future analytics).

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your personal data, and to object to processing. Because most of your data lives only on your device, you can exercise many of these rights directly by editing or clearing your local app data. For anything else, contact us at the address above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

Children

Nemeris is not intended for use by children under the age required to consent to data processing in their country.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
